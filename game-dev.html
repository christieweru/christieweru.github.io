My Projects




VOGUE!

For 10 weeks I built a Mock Game called VOGUE! This game follows the player into a world where they must become the next top fashion designer before the industry is taken over by the conglomerate brand CAPERE. The user is to go through different levels that increase in difficulty as they become powerful enough to beat the boss of CAPERE. They are able to unlock side quest and the trendiest outfits in VOGUE as they continue their quest through fashionlandia! Unlike other fashion games this one requires brains and brawn to reach the top. During the 10 weeks, I really got to hone in on my UI design skills, trying to find new ways to creatively display player health and items in a more aesthetic way.

Convalescence

Convalescence is a 2D action-RPG for PC, developed in just 10 weeks. Players embark on a journey to uncover the mysteries of the Fallen God, the bringer of discord. Along the way, they’ll master diverse combat strategies and battle through relentless hordes of enemies in a fight to restore harmony to the world of Convalescence once and for all. As one of the lead programmers, I was tasked with connecting the UI, coding core mechanics, and adding cut scenes that made the game flow smoothly.
